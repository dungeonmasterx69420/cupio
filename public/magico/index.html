The season in one paragraph: Orlando went 45–37, tied Philadelphia but lost the tiebreaker, dropped the 7/8 play-in game, then beat Charlotte to claim the 8 seed — a third straight playoff trip. They shocked top-seeded Detroit by going up 3–1, then it unraveled: a 24-point lead evaporated in Game 6 behind 23 straight missed shots, and Game 7 ended 116–94 in Detroit. Jamahl Mosley was let go days later; ex-Spurs assistant Sean Sweeney now runs the bench. The mandate for 2026–27 is simple — finish.
